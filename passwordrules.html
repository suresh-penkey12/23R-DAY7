PASSWORD RULES

Create strong passwords:

        1)Password security starts with creating a strong password. A strong password is:
        
        2)At least 12 characters long but 14 or more is better.
        
        3)A combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
        
        4)Not a word that can be found in a dictionary or the name of a person, character, product, or organization.
        
        5)Significantly different from your previous passwords.
        
        6)Easy for you to remember but difficult for others to guess. Consider using a memorable phrase like "6MonkeysRLooking^".
